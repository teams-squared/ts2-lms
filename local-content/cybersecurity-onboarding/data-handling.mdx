# Data Handling & Privacy

Handling data responsibly is a core responsibility for every person at Teams Squared. This document explains our data classification and what you can and cannot do with each type.

## Data Classification

| Level | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Public** | Safe to share externally | Marketing materials, published docs |
| **Internal** | For employees only | Company policies, internal tools |
| **Confidential** | Need-to-know basis | Customer data, financial records |
| **Restricted** | Strictly controlled | Credentials, PII, health data |

## Rules for Each Level

### Confidential & Restricted Data
- Never store in personal cloud storage (Google Drive personal, Dropbox, etc.)
- Never share via personal email
- Never discuss in public channels or in public places
- Access only what you need for your role — principle of least privilege

### Customer Data
- Treat all customer data as Confidential
- Do not copy production data to local machines or dev environments
- Anonymise or use synthetic data for testing
- Any breach or suspected exposure must be reported within 1 hour

## Acceptable Use of Company Systems

- Company devices and accounts are for work purposes
- You have no expectation of privacy on company systems
- Do not install unauthorised software on company devices
- Do not connect personal devices to internal systems without IT approval

## Data Retention

- Do not hoard data beyond its useful life
- Follow the retention schedules published by Legal
- When in doubt, ask `legal@teamssquared.com` before deleting or keeping records
