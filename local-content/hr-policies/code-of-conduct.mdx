# Code of Conduct

Teams Squared is committed to a workplace where everyone is treated with respect and dignity. This code applies to all employees, contractors, and partners.

## Core principles

1. **Respect** — Treat every person with courtesy regardless of role, background, or opinion.
2. **Integrity** — Be honest and transparent in your work and communications.
3. **Inclusion** — Welcome diverse perspectives and challenge your own assumptions.
4. **Accountability** — Own your mistakes, learn from them, and move forward.

## Unacceptable behaviour

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, or sexual orientation
- Sharing confidential information externally without authorisation
- Falsifying records, expenses, or time sheets

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. Report to your manager or HR directly
2. Use the anonymous reporting form in the People portal
3. Contact the Ethics Hotline at ethics@teamssquared.com

All reports are treated confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## Consequences

Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity. Serious violations (fraud, harassment) may be referred to law enforcement.
