# Remote Work Policy

Teams Squared operates as a **remote-first** company. This policy outlines expectations for remote employees.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for remote work. Contractors follow the terms of their individual agreements.

## Core Hours

You are expected to be reachable during **core hours: 10am–3pm in your local timezone**, Monday to Friday.

Outside core hours, async communication is preferred — respond within one business day.

## Equipment & Security

### Required setup

- Company-issued laptop with full-disk encryption enabled
- VPN connected when accessing internal systems

```bash
# Check VPN status on macOS
scutil --nc list | grep VPN

# Connect to VPN (example with Tailscale)
tailscale up --accept-routes
```

### Home office security

- Use WPA3 or WPA2 WiFi (never open networks)
- Keep router firmware updated
- Use a privacy screen in public spaces

## Communication Standards

| Situation | Tool |
|-----------|------|
| Quick questions | Slack (DM or channel) |
| Decisions / async updates | Notion or Linear |
| Team syncs | Google Meet |
| Urgent issues | Phone call |

## Travel & In-Person Time

- Quarterly all-hands: attendance expected (travel expensed)
- Team offsites: optional but encouraged
- Co-working space budget: $300/month if desired

## Expenses

Submit remote work expenses monthly via the Expense portal. Eligible expenses:
- Home internet (up to $60/month)
- Coworking space membership
- Peripheral equipment under $200 (pre-approved)
