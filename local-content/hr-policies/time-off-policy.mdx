# Time Off Policy

## Annual leave

All full-time employees receive **28 days** of paid annual leave per year (pro-rated for part-time and mid-year starters). Leave accrues from your start date.

Up to **5 days** may be carried over to the following year. Any unused leave beyond 5 days is forfeited on 1 January.

## Sick leave

You have **10 days** of paid sick leave per year. For absences over 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required. Extended illness may be covered under our long-term disability policy — contact HR for details.

## Public holidays

We observe all national public holidays in your country of employment. If a public holiday falls on a weekend, the following Monday is taken as a substitute day.

## How to request leave

1. Log in to the People portal
2. Navigate to **My Time → Request Leave**
3. Select dates and leave type
4. Submit — your manager will be notified automatically

Please give **at least 2 weeks notice** for annual leave longer than 3 days. Short-notice requests are accommodated where possible.

## Special leave

| Type | Entitlement |
|------|-------------|
| Bereavement (immediate family) | 5 days paid |
| Bereavement (extended family) | 2 days paid |
| Jury duty | Full pay for duration |
| Volunteering | 2 days paid per year |
| Moving house | 1 day paid |

For parental leave (maternity, paternity, adoption), refer to the separate Parental Leave Policy or contact HR.
