# Welcome to Teams Squared

We're thrilled to have you on the team. This guide covers everything you need to hit the ground running.

## Your first day checklist

- [ ] Set up your company email and Slack
- [ ] Complete IT security training (link in your welcome email)
- [ ] Book a 1-on-1 with your manager for this week
- [ ] Join the `#general` and `#your-team` Slack channels
- [ ] Read the [HR Policies](/docs/hr-policies/code-of-conduct) document

## Who to contact

| Need | Contact |
|------|---------|
| IT issues | it-support@teamssquared.com |
| HR questions | people@teamssquared.com |
| Payroll | finance@teamssquared.com |
| Your manager | Check your welcome email |

## Key tools

We use the following tools day-to-day:

- **Slack** — team communication
- **Linear** — issue tracking
- **GitHub** — source code
- **Notion** — project documentation
- **Google Workspace** — email, calendar, docs

## Office hours

The office is open Monday–Friday, 8am–7pm. Remote work is fully supported — check with your manager for team-specific norms.
