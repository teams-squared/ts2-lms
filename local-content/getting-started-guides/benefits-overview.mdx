# Benefits Overview

Welcome to Teams Squared! Here's a summary of the benefits you're entitled to as an employee.

## Health Insurance

We offer comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage through our partner providers.

| Plan | Employee Cost | Family Cost |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| Basic | $0/mo | $120/mo |
| Standard | $40/mo | $280/mo |
| Premium | $80/mo | $420/mo |

Enrol within **30 days** of your start date via the HR portal.

## Pension / 401(k)

- Company matches up to **4%** of your base salary
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- Provider: Fidelity

## Stock Options

All full-time employees receive an options grant:
- **Cliff**: 1 year
- **Vesting**: Monthly over 4 years
- **Strike price**: Set at fair market value on grant date

## Wellness Budget

Each employee receives **$100/month** to spend on:
- Gym memberships
- Mental health apps (Calm, Headspace)
- Ergonomic equipment

Submit reimbursement requests through Expense portal with receipts.

## Learning & Development

- **$2,000/year** for courses, conferences, or books
- Access to O'Reilly Learning platform (unlimited)
- 2 paid days per quarter for professional development

## Equipment

On your first day you'll receive:
- MacBook Pro (M-series)
- External monitor (27")
- Mechanical keyboard + mouse of choice
