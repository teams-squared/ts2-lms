# Hiring Process

This guide covers the full hiring lifecycle for hiring managers.

## Opening a Role

1. Discuss headcount need with your VP during quarterly planning
2. Submit a **Role Requisition** in Lever (our ATS)
3. Recruiter will create the job post and begin sourcing within 5 business days

Required fields in the requisition:
- Job title and level (IC1–IC5 / M1–M3)
- Reporting line
- Location (remote / hybrid / office)
- Salary band (check Levels.fyi benchmarks + internal bands)

## Interview Process

Our standard process for IC roles (adjust as needed):

| Stage | Format | Duration | Owner |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| Recruiter screen | Phone | 30 min | Recruiter |
| Hiring manager screen | Video | 45 min | Hiring manager |
| Technical assessment | Take-home | 2–3 hours | TL |
| Technical interview | Pair session | 60 min | 2 engineers |
| Values interview | Video | 45 min | Cross-functional peer |
| References | Async | — | Recruiter |

## Scoring Rubric

Use a 1–4 scale for each competency:

- **4 — Strong yes**: Clearly exceeds bar
- **3 — Yes**: Meets bar with confidence
- **2 — Lean no**: Concerns outweigh positives
- **1 — Strong no**: Does not meet bar

Submit scores in Lever within 24 hours of your interview.

## Making an Offer

Once the panel reaches consensus (≥ 3 scores of 3+):

1. Recruiter confirms compensation with People Ops
2. Verbal offer extended by hiring manager
3. Written offer sent via DocuSign within 24 hours

```text
Offer template variables:
- {{candidate_name}}
- {{role_title}}
- {{start_date}}
- {{base_salary}}
- {{equity_grant}}
- {{signing_bonus}} (if applicable)
```

## Onboarding Handoff

Once offer is accepted:
- Add to Rippling (HRIS) with start date
- Assign onboarding buddy
- Create 30/60/90 day plan in Notion
