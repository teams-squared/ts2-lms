# Cybersecurity at Teams Squared

Welcome to the Cybersecurity onboarding course. As a team-augmentation company, we work inside client environments and handle sensitive client data every day. That makes security everyone's responsibility — not just the IT team's.

This course will walk you through everything you need to know before you start work. It is mandatory for all new team members and should be completed during your first week.

## Why Security Matters Here

Teams Squared operates in a fully remote, cloud-first model. We don't have a physical office network to fall back on for protection — the security of client data depends entirely on each team member doing the right thing on their device, on their connection, and in their day-to-day habits.

A single incident — a weak password, a wrong attachment, a screen left visible in a café — can expose client data, breach contractual obligations, and cause serious reputational harm.

> **Remember:** You are often working inside a client's systems. A security failure on your device is a security failure for them too.

## Our Information Security Management System (ISMS)

Teams Squared maintains a formal **Information Security Management System (ISMS)** aligned to the **ISO/IEC 27001** standard. This means our security policies are documented, reviewed, and enforced — not just aspirational guidelines.

The ISMS is overseen by the **ISMS Owner (COO)** and managed day-to-day by the **Cybersecurity Lead and Team**.

## Your Responsibilities

Every team member is expected to:

- Read and comply with all security policies
- Complete this onboarding course before accessing any client systems
- Keep your device and credentials secure at all times
- Report anything suspicious immediately — no matter how small it seems
- Ask the Cybersecurity Team if you are ever unsure what to do

You are responsible for understanding and applying these policies. Ignorance of the rules is not a defence.
