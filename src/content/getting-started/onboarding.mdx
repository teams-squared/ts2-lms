# Onboarding Checklist

Welcome aboard! Follow this checklist to get set up during your first week.

## Day 1 - Getting Started

- [ ] Complete HR paperwork and benefits enrollment
- [ ] Set up your Microsoft 365 account
- [ ] Install required software (see Engineering docs for developers)
- [ ] Join the relevant Teams channels
- [ ] Meet your team lead and buddy

## Day 2-3 - Learning the Ropes

- [ ] Review company values and culture documentation
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training
- [ ] Set up your development environment (if applicable)
- [ ] Read through relevant documentation for your role

## Week 1 - Settling In

- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with key stakeholders
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with current projects
- [ ] Complete your first small task or contribution
- [ ] Provide feedback on the onboarding experience
