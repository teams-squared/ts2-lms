# Welcome to Teams Squared

Welcome to the Teams Squared internal documentation portal! This is your central resource for all company guides, processes, and best practices.

## What You'll Find Here

This portal is organized into categories to help you quickly find what you need:

- **Getting Started** - Onboarding materials and introductory guides
- **Engineering** - Technical documentation, coding standards, and deployment guides
- **Management** - Leadership resources and team policies (restricted access)

## How to Use This Portal

1. **Browse by category** - Use the sidebar or home page to explore documentation by topic
2. **Search** - Use the search bar to find specific documents across all categories
3. **Role-based access** - Some documents may be restricted based on your role

## Need Help?

If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out to your team lead or the documentation team. We're always looking to improve and expand our docs.
