# Budget Guidelines

This document outlines the financial guidelines and budget management procedures for Teams Squared leadership.

## Annual Budget Cycle

| Phase | Timeline | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Planning | October - November | Department heads submit budget proposals |
| Review | December | Finance team reviews and consolidates |
| Approval | January | Board approves final budget |
| Execution | February - December | Departments operate within approved budgets |

## Spending Authority

| Role | Approval Limit | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Team Lead | Up to $1,000 | Pre-approved categories only |
| Manager | Up to $5,000 | Must be within department budget |
| Director | Up to $25,000 | Requires finance partner review |
| VP | Up to $100,000 | Requires CFO notification |
| C-Suite | Unlimited | Board notification for >$500K |

## Expense Categories

### Approved Categories
- Software and tools
- Training and development
- Team events and morale
- Travel (with pre-approval)
- Hardware and equipment

### Requires Special Approval
- Consulting and contractors
- Marketing spend
- Capital expenditure
- Multi-year commitments

## Reporting

Monthly budget reports are due by the 5th of each month. Use the standard reporting template available in the Finance portal.
