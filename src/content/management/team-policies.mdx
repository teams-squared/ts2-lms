# Team Policies

This document contains management-level policies and guidelines for team leads and managers.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted quarterly. Managers should:

1. **Prepare feedback** based on documented accomplishments and areas for growth
2. **Schedule 30-minute 1:1s** with each direct report
3. **Use the standard template** from the HR portal
4. **Submit completed reviews** within 5 business days of the review period end

## Time Off Approval

- Approve time off requests within 48 hours
- Ensure adequate team coverage before approving
- Encourage team members to use their full PTO allocation
- Holiday blackout periods must be communicated at least 30 days in advance

## Hiring Process

1. Submit a headcount request through the HR portal
2. Write a job description using the company template
3. Conduct at least 3 rounds of interviews
4. Make offers within 48 hours of final interview
5. Ensure a smooth onboarding experience for new hires

## Budget Guidelines

- Department budgets are reviewed quarterly
- Purchases over $5,000 require VP approval
- Software licenses must be approved by IT
- Travel expenses follow the standard company policy

## Escalation Path

| Issue Type | First Contact | Escalation |
|-----------|--------------|------------|
| Technical | Engineering Lead | CTO |
| HR/People | HR Business Partner | VP People |
| Budget | Finance Partner | CFO |
| Customer | Account Manager | VP Sales |
