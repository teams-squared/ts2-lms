# Security Onboarding Checklist

Complete every item on this checklist before accessing any client systems. If you are unsure how to complete any step, contact the Cybersecurity Team at **cybersecurity@teamsquared.io**.

## Course Completion

- [ ] Read [Cybersecurity at Teams Squared](/security/cybersecurity-intro)
- [ ] Read [Passwords & Access](/security/passwords-and-access)
- [ ] Read [Devices & Remote Work](/security/devices-and-remote-work)
- [ ] Read [Data & Communications](/security/data-and-communications)
- [ ] Read [Incident Reporting](/security/incident-reporting)

## 1Password Setup

- [ ] Received your 1Password invitation email
- [ ] Created your 1Password account with a strong master password (12+ characters, meets complexity requirements)
- [ ] Enabled MFA on your 1Password account
- [ ] Saved your emergency recovery codes in a secure, offline location
- [ ] Installed the 1Password browser extension on your company device
- [ ] Installed the 1Password desktop app on your company device
- [ ] Moved any existing work credentials into 1Password and removed them from browsers or documents

## MFA Setup

- [ ] Installed the recommended authenticator app on your phone (see your onboarding email for the recommended app)
- [ ] Enabled MFA on your Microsoft 365 account
- [ ] Enabled MFA on 1Password
- [ ] Enabled MFA on any other company-assigned platforms as instructed

## Device Setup

- [ ] Received your company-issued laptop from the Cybersecurity Team
- [ ] Confirmed full disk encryption is active (do not attempt to disable it)
- [ ] Confirmed Bitdefender Endpoint Security is installed and running
- [ ] Confirmed you have not installed any unapproved software
- [ ] Confirmed automatic screen lock is enabled on your device

## VPN Setup

- [ ] Received your VPN credentials during onboarding
- [ ] Installed and tested the company VPN on your device
- [ ] Confirmed you can connect to the VPN before accessing Teams Squared systems

## Work Location & Compliance

- [ ] Declared your primary remote working location to Teams Squared
- [ ] Understand that working from a different country requires prior written approval
- [ ] Understand that working away from your primary location for more than 20 hours/week requires prior written approval

## Policies Acknowledged

- [ ] Understand that company devices must not be shared with or used by anyone else
- [ ] Understand that USB storage and external drives are blocked by default
- [ ] Understand that client data must stay within approved systems and client-specific repositories
- [ ] Understand that AI tools must not be used with client data without explicit approval
- [ ] Understand that printing requires prior written approval
- [ ] Understand how to report a security incident (email cybersecurity@teamsquared.io)

## Final Step

Once you have completed all items above, notify the Cybersecurity Team that your security onboarding is complete.

**Email:** cybersecurity@teamsquared.io
**Subject:** `Onboarding – Security Setup Complete – [Your Name]`

> **You must not access any client systems until this checklist is fully complete and acknowledged.**
